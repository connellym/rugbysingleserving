Glossarry

  1. Hooker-Hooks the ball backwards in the scrum keeps the crew together
  2. Forward-Fat guys who push the ball forward to make their counter part backs look good
  3. Backs-The skinnier lighter version of the back who gets hit less, but runs more
  4. Lineout-Lifting compettion between the opposing teams
  5. Maul-Forwards pushing the ball forward against oposing forwards
  6. Pack-An adjective for forwards
  7. Advantage-When one team has a penalty,play occurs until the team that committed the penalty gets the ball
  8. Forward Pass-Illegal you can only throw the ball backwards
  9. Drop Goal- When a team is too weak to score Try's they kick the ball between the goal posts
  10. Conversion- extra point for scoring try's
  11. Try-A score
  12. Ruck-Forwards from both teams fighting over the ball
  13. Red Card-Kicked out for being a dick to the other team
  14. Try Line The border between a boot and failure
  15. Touch Judge-Weakest link who marks where the ball goes out of bounds
  16. Yellow card- Not as bad as a red
  17. Sin-Bin-A humiliating place where players pulling illegal moves must sit out
  18. Beer-The only cure for a post rugby game/The nectar of the god/The only thing keeping forwards from taking over the world

THE PLAYS

There are several plays involved, if viewing or playing rugby. Rucks,Mauls,Scrums, and normal plays are what most people should be interested in. A ruck is when a player gets tackled by an opposing team member. When the player is tackled he forms a gate. This means opposing players must step over and not from the sides of his body. The opposing team pushes the other team off the ball. The other team is allowed to defend and push the opposite way.
